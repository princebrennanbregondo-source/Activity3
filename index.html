The Struggler’s Path: Information Technology

They say we all have that one moment that cuts deeper than steel. For me, it wasn’t fate, it wasn’t destiny — it was a single right-click.

I’m Prince Brennan Bregondo, 18, walking the halls of the University of Cebu – Main Campus, BSIT-J. This path wasn’t my parents’ choice, it wasn’t even mine. But the world forced my hand, and I carved it open myself.

The Spark

At 9, lost in Roblox, I right-clicked. “Inspect.” The void cracked open. Code — raw, merciless.

I didn’t understand, but I knew one thing: if the world was built on this, then I’d wield it like a blade.

The Weight

IT wasn’t a dream. It was a burden — heavy, sharp, and mine to carry. While others saw a career, I saw a battlefield of logic, firewalls, and endless nights.

The Hunt

Programming, networking, databases, cybersecurity — they’re not just subjects. They’re scars, each one carved deeper as I grind toward mastery. I’m not here for grades. I’m here to sharpen my blade.

The Code I Bleed

Knowledge isn’t comfort. Knowledge is a weapon. In a world that tears you apart, I’ll forge my code to strike back.

Eyes on the Void

The nights will break me. The bugs will mock me. The failures will haunt me.

But I’ll keep moving. Because I’m not here to play safe. I’m here to struggle, to bleed, to carve my mark.

This isn’t the end. It’s the first swing.
